Congratulations!

We are very excited to inform you that you have been accepted to the second edition of HackShanghai, which will take place on November 7-8, 2015 at New York University Shanghai!

For the second installment of the largest hackathon in China, you are one of the 250 selected participants, out of hundreds of great students from top universities in the world.

A few things to keep in mind:

Travel:

  • Based on our research of train tickets, we have decided to reimburse your ticket for up to a total sum of ¥400.00 (CNY) . Any additional cost is not liable to be covered by us.

  • We will take care of reimbursements as quickly and efficiently as we can, however we need your help to streamline this process. Please look at http://www.hackshanghai.com/reimbursement for detailed information on the documents you need to bring to be reimbursed for your trip. WE CAN NOT BE HELD RESPONSIBLE FOR REIMBURSING INDIVIDUALS WHO DO NOT PROVIDE THE REQUIRED DOCUMENTS AND INFORMATION IN A TIMELY MANNER.

Registration:

  • You need to arrive at NYU Shanghai on November 7th between 10 AM - 12 PM for the registration process.

  • You need to have your original National ID (or Passport) and two separate copies of it with you, otherwise YOU WILL BE UNABLE TO TAKE PART IN THE EVENT.

  • The NYU Shanghai Academic Building is located at 1555 Century Ave, Pudong, Shanghai, China, 200122 . It can be easily reached from the Metro stop at Century Avenue on lines 2, 4, 6 and 9. If you wish to take a taxi, you may print this taxi card to show to your driver, http://shanghai.nyu.edu/sites/default/files/media/Campus-Taxi-Card_0.pdf . For more information and a detailed map, check http://shanghai.nyu.edu/about/contact .

Accommodation:

  • We will not provide accommodation outside the time of the event.

Rules:

  • Make sure to read through the HackShanghai rules and code of conduct before the event, they can be found at http://www.hackshanghai.com/rules and http://www.hackshanghai.com/conduct respectively. You will be asked to comply with these documents and also sign a Participation Form upon entry to the hackathon.

For more information please visit the FAQ section of our website, http://www.hackshanghai.com/ , or drop us an email at hi@hackshanghai.com and we will get back to you at the earliest.

IMPORTANT: If you agree with the terms of this email and plan to come to HackShanghai this November, we need to receive your written confirmation as a reply to this email within the next two days. Should we not receive a reply from you by 23:59 PM CST, October 30th, 2015, we will consider that you do not wish to take part and THE CONTENTS OF THIS EMAIL WILL BECOME VOID. Otherwise, it would be unfair towards all of the other applicants that we see ourselves forced to reject. We thank you for your understanding.

Congratulations once again on your acceptance! We look forward to seeing you here in November!


恭喜你！

我们非常高兴地通知你，你已被选入了第二届创客上海。创客上海2015将于 2015年11月7日 至8日 在上海纽约大学举行。

在第二届中国最大的创客比赛中，你从上百位申请者中脱颖而出，成为1/250的参赛者。当你置身于繁华的上海浦东新区时，你将与从中国各地和世界各地赶来的其他249名顶尖学生程序员相会。

注意事项：

交通：

  • 基于我们对于交通费用的调查，我们将最多报销 ¥400.00 RMB 的交通费用。任何超过此价格的部分将不由我们承担。

  • 我们将尽快高效地处理报销，但是我们需要你的帮助使该过程流水线化。请阅读 http://www.hackshanghai.com/reimbursement 并知晓报销的细节，以及你所需要携带的文件。 我们对于不能及时提供有效所需证件和信息的参赛者不承担任何报销责任。


  • 为确保你能及时登记，请在 11月7日 早10点-12点到达上海。

  • 你需要持有你的身份证原件，以及两份复印件（分印与两张纸上）以确保参赛。

  • 上海纽约大学教学楼位于 中国上海浦东新区世纪大道1555号，邮编200122。地铁2,4,6,9号线均可到达，站点为世纪大道站。如果你要乘坐出租车，你可以将此地址卡打印出来并出示给司机： http://shanghai.nyu.edu/sites/default/files/media/Campus-Taxi-Card_0.pdf . 更多有关地址和地图的信息请点击： http://shanghai.nyu.edu/about/contact .
