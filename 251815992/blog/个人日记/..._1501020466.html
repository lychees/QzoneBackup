Greeting!
I am supposed to transfer to MTV in the coming quarter. But there are few jobs that interest me in headquarter at this moment.
I started searching for jobs on go/grow, and was attracted immediately by this opportunity.

In google, I am working in Google Data Warehouse team, first be a part of DIA and now belongs to CDS.
In GDW team, we run several pipelines that drive business functions for analytic, finance, sales and marketing.

My duty is:
- Actively resolving Finance BI asks and client issues.
- Building helpful tools in order to make on call life easier.
- Thinking about how to optimize the performance in different ways and enhance our various infrastructure.

However, my enthusiasm in game industry is consistent.
I used to hack, modify and write agents on different console games include Sokoban, Snake and Pac-Man by my own since childhood.
Before joining Google, I worked in a game company in Shanghai collaborating with several interns as a team to build a brand new project using cocos2d-x. I also joined in an online educational company in Beijing, writing educational articles about game AI and
building educational video game for kids to learn basic software programming concepts.

I felt fortunate to witness the Future of Go Summit here in Wuzhen, China, a historic event of the ancient game Go on May 27th. It was so impressive how technology can influence and change the human mind and the human activities. I do believe what we do in Deepmind will be the foot stone in future human civilization. And I really want to be a part of it, be a part of this ongoing history.
