\item \textbf{\href{ https://github.com/aswmtjdsj/gopool }{Gpool}} Nov. 2013 - Oct. 2014
\begin{itemize}
\item{\textbf{Introduction} Bitcoin is a peer-to-peer payment system invented by Satoshi Nakamoto. Users can transact directly without needing an intermediary. Transactions are verified by network nodes and recorded in a public distributed ledger called the block chain. A mining pool is the pooling of resources by miners, who share their processing power over a network, to split the reward equally, according to the amount of work they contributed to solving a block. A "share" is awarded to members of the mining pool who present a valid proof of work that their miner solved. GPool is a multipool based on Pay-per-last-N-shares rules for XPM-like altcoins. (DTC, XPM, MMC and etc.) It is \textbf{the first profitable project} I have ever involved.

Unfortunately Gpool suffered twice serious hitting before closing. The first is the rising of GPU-miners for XPM and the second is the hacker invasion on two main Bitcoin exchanges.}
\vspace{2pt}
\item{\textbf{Related Technology} p2pool, Go, Semantic-UI, Javascript}
\end{itemize}
