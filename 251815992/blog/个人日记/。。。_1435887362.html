Why did the owner of the manuscript do this？
He did this because he understands data as well as books.
Now the thing to do with books, If you want to ensure their long-term utility is to hide them away in closets,
and let very few people look at them.

The thing to do with data, if you want it to survive,
is to let it out, and have everybody have it with as little control on that data as possible.
And that's what he did.

这份手稿的主人为什么要这样做呢？
因为他理解数据和书籍的本质。
对于书籍，如果要确保他们可以长期流传
就要锁在柜子里，限制阅读人数。

而对于数据，如果你希望他可以存活，
就要让他公开出去，让每个人都拥有一份，而且限制尽可能少。
这就是他所做的。
