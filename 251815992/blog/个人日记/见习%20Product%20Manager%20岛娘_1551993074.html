岛娘案：
Running a startup is fantastic. You can never go back to Google once you have run a company.
That's true. 如果你想最大限度的发挥你的能力的全部，开一家新创公司吧！
成为新创公司 CEO 的一个好处是，你可以遇到很多以为你和他一个 Level 的有趣的人，与他们对话，并从他们身上获取新知。
而在我所从事的领域，更是每天都会有新的 Idea，每月都截然不同。

上文说到，我最近最大的工作就是参与设计并推进智能签名。 https://github.com/smart-signature
和其他产品经历不同的是，我具有编写代码的能力。我可以把产品的内核表述为智能合约，把产品的线框图表述为 Web Prototype。
并且通过带着这些 Prototype 和更多的人交流，我确实收获了很多 Feedback。

而最近这段时间最大的收获，就是读到了一篇名为 Status as a Service 的文章。
https://www.eugenewei.com/blog/2019/2/19/status-as-a-service

值得一提的是，在中文世界， 独立博客退化 的今天，已经鲜有人能够，动辄写出一份 30000 字的博文了。
—— 我无疑发现了一片巨大的宝藏。

I live in San Francisco. Before that I lived in Los Angeles, New York, Seattle, and Chicago, where I grew up.

Most of my professional career has been spent at consumer internet companies. The world wide web was just heating up when I finished my undergrad education, and like many grads from Stanford, tech was always top of mind. I started off at Amazon.com and was there for seven years working on all sorts of things, but mostly product. I left Amazon to be a filmmaker, went to editing school at The Edit Center in NYC, then to UCLA Film School in their graduate directing program. But tech sucked me back in after just one year in film school.

That summer I joined the company that would become Hulu, leading the product, design, editorial, and marketing teams. In 2011 I formed a startup called Erly with a few friends. We were purchased in 2012 by Airtime, and I left that in late 2012. I was the head of product at Flipboard for two years, then the Head of Video at Oculus, which I left in July 2017. I'm now working on some of my own ideas, most of which sit at the intersection of media and technology.


先来介绍这篇文章的作者， Eugene Wei。
根据 Blog 里的介绍，EW 应该是一位居住在旧金山的美籍华人，并且在多家互联网公司工作过。包括 Amazon、Hulu、Flipboard、Oculus。其中在 2011 年自己也做过新创，并且成功得到 acquisition。中间还去 NYC 和 UCLA 学过两年电影。可见他的涉猎范围绝对不仅仅只有产品经理。

也正因为如此丰富的经历，在这篇文章中，作者作出了一个大胆的类比，将用户在社交网络的行为，与 ICO 首次代币发行进行比较，并且得出人们在社交网络中的行为，都是在搜寻一种叫做 Social Capital 的隐性资产。
而把这种隐性资产量化并扩大，不就是我现在设计的产品，正在做的事情么。
